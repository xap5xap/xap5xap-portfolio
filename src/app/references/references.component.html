Photo Inspiration

Shows best photos in Flickr

This is a mobile app that shows the best photos in Flickr. Search photos by day, analyze the settings the photo was taken, such as the aperture, the shutter speed, the focal length, the camera model, the lens, among others.
No logging to Flickr required! Just open the app and you have the best photos, every day.

This project was developed with Angular2, Ionic2 and the Flickr API.
